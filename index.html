Full Grab Rail Specification.
Every Configuration.

 

Marine grade stainless. Every mounting style, tubing diameter, and angle you need.

Marine Grade Stainless  ·  Precision Cast  ·  CAD Ready

 

All White Water grab rails are precision cast in marine grade stainless steel, the marine industry corrosion standard.

The lineup covers top-mount, stud-mount, offset stud, and folding configurations across tubing diameters of 3/4″ and 7/8″. Angled variants are available at 50° and 60°. Threaded-end versions eliminate stud interference in tight installations. Full CAD drawings available upon request.

If your application requires a configuration outside the standard lineup, our engineering team works directly from your CAD or sample.
